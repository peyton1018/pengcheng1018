About Me

杜鹏程，来自中国湖北省武汉市，东京工艺大学大学院交互媒体专业硕士学位，目前正在攻读博士学位，研究方向为虚拟现实。作为新媒体艺术领域的学者和创作者，他在数字媒体技术、人机交互等方面有着深厚的研究背景，并发表了大量论文和研究成果。杜鹏程对欧美和日本方向的数字媒体及人机交互研究尤为擅长，精通美国STAM方向的专业申请。通过不断的学术研究与创作，他致力于推动数字艺术与技术的融合，为学生提供专业的指导和学术支持，帮助他们在全球化的数字艺术和技术领域中找到自己的发展方向。
Pengcheng Du, from Wuhan, Hubei Province, China, has a master's degree in Interactive Media from the Graduate School of Tokyo Polytechnic University, and is currently pursuing a doctoral degree in Virtual Reality. As a scholar and creator in the field of new media art, he has a deep research background in digital media technology and human-computer interaction, and has published numerous papers and research results. Du Pengcheng is particularly good at digital media and human-computer interaction research in the direction of Europe, America and Japan, and is proficient in professional application in the direction of STAM in the United States. Through continuous academic research and creation, he is committed to promoting the integration of digital art and technology, providing professional guidance and academic support for students to help them find their way in the globalised field of digital art and technology.
